Research Excellence Framework 2014

Some of the material on this project site has been submitted to the UK Research Excellence Framework (REF) 2014, which is assessing the quality of research published between the 1 January 2008 and 31 December 2013.

The publication dates of all relevant material here can be verified by reference to the project team's announcements on the Early Music Theory Blog, which can also be accessed from the project home page. In addition, a date-stamped version of the project, as at December 2013, has been archived especially for this purpose, accessible from the link below. In this archived version, the Twitter feed provided on the home page does, however, remain live and up to date.
