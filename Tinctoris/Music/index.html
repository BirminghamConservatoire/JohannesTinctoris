In progress here is a critical edition, in the original notation, of all Tinctoris’s practical compositions, including those composed as illustrations in De arte contrapuncti. We have also edited selected works of Guillaume du Faÿ and Antoine Busnoys as test material for our software tool that automatically interprets imperfection and alteration.
