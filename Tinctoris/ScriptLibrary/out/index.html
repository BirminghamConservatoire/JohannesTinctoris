mensural-rhythm.js

This code takes mensural MEI and attempts to turn implicit duration into explicit duration, encoded as @dur.ges, @num, @numbase and @dur.quality. Input is assumed to have positions correct, dots classified and implicit mensuration resolved (although the system will attempt to guess major and minor modus from rests).
